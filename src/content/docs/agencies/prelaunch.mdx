---
title: "Pre-Launch Checklist"
description: "Everything to verify before a store goes live. Missing one of these means lost sales, lost data, or a lost client."
sidebar:
  order: 41
---

**1. Payment gateway is in live mode.** Test/sandbox credentials look similar to live. Place a real (small) test order and verify the charge appears in your processor's live dashboard.

**2. Place a complete test order as a customer.** From the frontend, in incognito, as a new customer. Verify the order appears in WooCommerce, the confirmation email arrives, and payment is captured.

**3. Shipping zones cover all destinations.** Test domestic and international. Check that "Rest of World" is configured or intentionally empty.

**4. Tax is calculating correctly.** Test orders for nexus states and non-nexus states. For EU: test domestic and cross-border. For B2B: test VAT number entry.

**5. Email delivery works.** Test with Gmail, Yahoo, and a corporate domain. If any fail, fix authentication before launch.

**6. SSL is active site-wide.** Every page HTTPS. No mixed content warnings.

**7. Checkout works on mobile.** Actual phone, not resized desktop browser.

**8. Backup is configured AND tested.** Run it, verify it completed, confirm you can restore from it.

**9. Site speed is acceptable.** Homepage, product page, cart — under 3-4 seconds on fresh browser.

**10. Legal pages exist.** Privacy Policy, Terms, Refund Policy — populated and linked from checkout.
